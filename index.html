Salary explorer

Region-adjusted pay bands built from ONS earnings data, so candidates and employers start from the same numbers. Pick a region and see indicative brackets by level.

Figures are indicative full-time gross annual pay, modelled on ONS ASHE regional data. Actual pay varies by sector — finance and tech typically run 20–60% above these bands, hospitality and retail below them. Executive figures reflect large/mid-size employers; small-company leadership pay is often lower.

Why Rolexa is different

Most UK job boards were built for one slice of the market — graduate schemes, executive search, or blue-collar shifts. Rolexa is built to be the one place that covers the whole career, with the transparency 2026 candidates actually expect.
